About the Site

College Classified is a site created by high school students, for high school students. Whether it's finding scholarships to apply to, figuring out you perfecct college fit through a quiz, or managing your deadlines calender, College Classifed is here to make the college admissions process as seamless as possible.

About the team

This site was made by the Girls Who Code Young Women Tech Leaders at the Queens Central Y. As young high school students themselves, the College Classifed team wanted to make applying to college accessible to other young girls. Throughout the winter and spring of 2017, they and their two Girls Who Code facilitators worked long and hard each week to singlehandly code the website you are reading now from scratch. They hope to inspire and encourage more girls in STEM and encourage girls to apply to college.
